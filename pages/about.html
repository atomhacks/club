Bronx Science Hackers is the premier programming club at Bronx Science, a specialized high school in New York City. We help club members create projects in areas that they're interested, organize workshops and events, and host bimonthly demo days. If you're a Bronx Science student and you're interested in joining, you can submit your email address through the form on the homepage, or send us an email through the contact page.
